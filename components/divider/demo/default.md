<cn>
### 水平分割线
默认为水平分割线，可在中间加入文字。
</cn>
<en>
### Horizontal Divider
Default is a horizontal divider, with text that can be added in the middle.
</en>

```vue
<template>
  <div>
    <p>See the light through the mist.！</p>
    <Divider />
    <p>See the light through the mist.！</p>
    <Divider>Text</Divider>
    <p>See the light through the mist.！</p>
    <Divider dashed />
    <p>See the light through the mist.！</p>
  </div>
</template>
```
